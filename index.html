About me

I'm currently studying International Game Architecture and Design at Breda Univesity of Applied Sciences. I'm involved in the development of gameplay, tools and engines such as Lux Engine, targeting Windows and PlayStation 4. I have experience with project management and development pipelines as a Scrum Master and engine programmer. I contribute to open-source projects, such as GLTF and Otter Space, on GitHub.
