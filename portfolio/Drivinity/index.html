Description

  • Team: Godspeed (22 people)
  • Role: Gameplay & Tools Programming
  • Development: 8 weeks
  • Published: June 28 2018
  • Platforms: Windows 10, PlayStation 4
  • Software: Visual Studio, CMate, Lux Engine, Unity
  • Languages: C++, C#, Batch, CMake, JSON, GLTF
  • Itch.io

Drivinity is a fast-paced anti-gravity racing game where Egyptian Gods collide in an epic race to decide once and for all who is superior. Players can choose between two different Gods and two tracks to race in a futuristic Egyptian city where technology has mixed with ancient Egypt.

Drivinity was developed during the last 8 weeks of my second year at Breda University of Applied Sciences. After being greenlit the previous semester, we had a student team of 5 Artists, 6 Designers, and 10 Programmers.
