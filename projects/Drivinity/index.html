Description

Players can choose between two different Gods and two tracks to race in a futuristic Egyptian city where technology has mixed with ancient Egypt. Drivinity was developed during the last 8 weeks of my second year at Breda University of Applied Sciences. After being greenlit the previous semester, we had a student team of 5 Artists, 6 Designers, and 10 Programmers.
