  • Jasper Drescher

Roles Engine Programmer & Scrum Master
Team Godspeed
Release date June 28 2018
Duration February 2018 - May 2018
Platforms Windows, PlayStation 4
Languages C++, C#, Batch, CMake, GLTF

Description

As an Engine Programmer my job was to develop the architecture of the object-oriented game engine, and to implement a physically based rendering pipeline with GLTF. During the first 8 weeks of development, I managed the Scrum board and iterated on the design and implementation of most low-level architecture and classes. Other work, such as the renderer and custom memory allocator was done by fellow programming students.
