Engine Unreal Engine
Platform Windows
Language C++

Description

Stellar Survivor is our first-person, co-op photo-realistic survival game. Players can play alone or together with friends on Steam fighting off hordes of alien enemies that try to destroy the powercore the players have to defend. Players do so with the use of weapons with different characteristics and traps that, when put to good use, can take down these aliens effectively.
